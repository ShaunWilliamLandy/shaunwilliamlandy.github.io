About Me

I'm a developer with 9 years of programming experience. Originally from Liverpool, I moved to Bristol to study Games Technology at the The University of the West of England. Later moving to Swindon to work in full stack web development.

My developer journey started with me learning C++ at university which lead to working with Unreal 4 (blueprints), Directx11 and Unity3D (C#). Strongly focused on Unity3D, I developed with Vive, Oculus and even got some hands on experience with the PS4 Devkits and PSVR. Post-univesity I moved into a full stack web developer role which involved picking up skills such as Ruby on Rails and React along the way.

Other than development and programming, it isn't too hard to guess that I have an interest in games. Some of my notable favourites: Detroit: Become Human, Until Dawn, Life is Strange, LittleBigPlanet, Dishonored, Stardew Valley, Dead by Daylight, Horizon Zero Dawn, Beat Saber. Outside of games, I enjoy climbing/bouldering, kayaking and other adventurous outdoor activities such as canyoning.
